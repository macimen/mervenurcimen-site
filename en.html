About me

I am a Clinical Psychologist, graduated with honours from the University of Bergamo and currently specialising in Neo-Existential Psychotherapy at the School of Neo-Existential Psychotherapy (S.P.Es.) in Naples.

My professional experience has developed in clinical, educational and associative contexts, working with children, adolescents and adults, both in person and online.

I have worked in multicultural environments and participated in psychological support projects for people with migrant backgrounds.

As a founding member of the ITAPSI Association, I contribute to the promotion of psychological and psychosocial initiatives dedicated to community wellbeing.

My approach

My approach is mainly based on existential psychotherapy, which considers each person in their uniqueness and in the complexity of their experience.

I believe that people should not have to adapt themselves to a therapeutic model; rather, the therapeutic path should adapt to the person, integrating, when necessary, contributions from different psychological approaches.
